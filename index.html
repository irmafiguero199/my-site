A Legacy of Hope Since Day One

What started as a small family gathering in Sanford, Florida, has blossomed into a global movement. The Amsler Family Foundation was born out of a simple observation: that resources exist, but they are often disconnected from the people who need them most.

We believe in the power of "Human-Centric Development." This means we don't just send checks; we build relationships. We spend time on the ground, talking to local leaders, understanding the cultural nuances, and co-creating solutions that the community can own and sustain long after our initial project is complete.

Our commitment to EIN 81-0854933 compliance and 501(c)(3) status ensures that every resource is managed with the highest degree of ethical integrity.

Success Stories

Real people. Real impact. Explore how your support changes lives.

A New Future for Maria

Maria, a bright student from an underserved neighborhood, had the talent but lacked the funds for higher education. Through our **Educational Scholarship Fund**, she completed her degree in Nursing and is now serving her local clinic, giving back to the community that raised her.

Healing Through Community

In 2025, our **Wellness Mobile Clinic** reached over 5,000 residents in rural areas who previously had no access to preventive screenings. By detecting early signs of chronic disease, we saved lives and reduced local healthcare costs significantly.

Our Strategic Pillars

A deep dive into how we operate and where we focus our resources.

Academic Excellence

We provide more than books. Our program includes high-speed internet hubs, mentorship pairings with industry leaders, and vocational training for those entering the modern workforce.

Preventive Health

Focusing on mental health, nutrition, and early screenings. We believe a community's greatest wealth is its health, and we work tirelessly to protect it.

Social Leadership

Training the next generation of non-profit leaders. We provide grants to small local organizations that align with our mission of social inclusion and ethical governance.

Transparency & Privacy

Data Protection

Amsler Family Foundation (AFF) follows global standards for data privacy. We do not sell, share, or redistribute any donor or volunteer information. Any personal data collected is used solely for foundation-related communication.

Tax-Exempt Status

We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by US law. Our EIN 81-0854933 is available for verification via the IRS official database.
